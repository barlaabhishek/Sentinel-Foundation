# Governance

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** Founding Edition (2026)

---

## Purpose

Governance defines how Sentinel intends to make decisions, evolve responsibly, and preserve its founding principles across generations.

---

## Governance Principles

- Decisions should be documented.
- Authority should always carry accountability.
- Long-term institutional trust outweighs short-term advantage.
- Significant constitutional changes should be transparent.
- Every generation should inherit Sentinel stronger than it was received.

---

This document represents the initial governance framework and will mature alongside the institution.
