# Sentinel Constitution

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** Founding Edition (2026)

---

## Purpose

The Sentinel Constitution defines the enduring principles that govern the institution.

Products, technologies, and markets may evolve.

These constitutional principles are intended to remain stable unless changed through deliberate and transparent revision.

---

## Constitutional Commitments

1. Sentinel exists to strengthen trustworthy coordination.
2. Human well-being takes precedence over organizational convenience.
3. Trust is earned through consistent action, not claims.
4. Decisions should favor long-term resilience over short-term optimization.
5. Systems should increase clarity, accountability, and responsible execution.
6. Every meaningful decision should be explainable.
7. Ethical responsibility extends beyond legal compliance.
8. Continuous learning is expected, but foundational integrity is non-negotiable.

---

## Scope

This Constitution serves as the highest guiding document for Sentinel.

Future documents, products, and governance should remain consistent with these principles.

---

This Constitution will mature alongside Sentinel while preserving its founding intent.
