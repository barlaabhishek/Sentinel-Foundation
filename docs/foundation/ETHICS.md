# Ethics

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** Founding Edition (2026)

---

## Purpose

Technology should increase human capability without reducing human dignity.

Sentinel's ethical framework exists to ensure that coordination systems strengthen trust, accountability, transparency, and responsible decision-making.

---

## Ethical Commitments

1. Human well-being comes before efficiency.
2. Transparency should replace unnecessary opacity.
3. AI should assist judgment, not replace responsibility.
4. Privacy must be respected by design.
5. Every system should be explainable to those affected by it.
6. Technology should reduce harm rather than merely optimize outcomes.
7. Long-term societal trust is more valuable than short-term commercial success.
