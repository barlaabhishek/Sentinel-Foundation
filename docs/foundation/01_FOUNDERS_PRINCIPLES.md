# Founder's Principles

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** Founding Edition (2026)

---

## Purpose

These principles define the standards expected of Sentinel's founder before they are expected of anyone else.

They are intended to guide decisions consistently through periods of uncertainty, growth, success, and failure.

They exist to provide continuity between Sentinel's mission and its actions.

---

## Core Principles

1. Truth before convenience.
2. Integrity before recognition.
3. Responsibility before authority.
4. Systems before ego.
5. Long-term trust before short-term gain.
6. Clarity before complexity.
7. Evidence before assumption.
8. Service before status.
9. Accountability before excuses.
10. Continuous learning without abandoning first principles.

---

These principles will continue evolving as Sentinel matures, while preserving their original intent.
