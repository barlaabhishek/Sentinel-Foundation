# Sentinel

> **Status:** Founding Edition (2026) • Pre-Product • Repository under active development

> **Sentinel is building trusted coordination infrastructure that enables complex organizations to coordinate work with greater clarity, accountability, and operational confidence.**

---

## Mission

Sentinel exists to improve coordination across the world's most critical operations by creating trusted infrastructure that strengthens clarity, accountability, and operational execution.

Healthcare is where this mission begins—not where it ends.

---

## Why Sentinel Exists

Modern organizations rarely fail because people lack ability.

They fail because information, responsibility, and execution become fragmented across people, teams, departments, and systems.

Sentinel exists to reduce that fragmentation through trusted coordination infrastructure.
